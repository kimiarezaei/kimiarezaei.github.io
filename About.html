About Me

Hi, I am Kimia Rezaei, a PhD candidate in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at University College Cork and the INFANT Research Centre. My research focuses on developing AI models for the automatic diagnosis of neonatal brain-related conditions by leveraging ECG signals as an alternative to EEG, using signal processing, machine learning and deep learning techniques.

Previously, I worked on machine learning approaches for analyzing brain-related medical images. I also have over five years of experience in the telecom industry, where I was part of a technical maintenance team.

I'm passionate about applying Artificial Intelligence in healthcare and enthusiastic about exploring its potential across other domains.
